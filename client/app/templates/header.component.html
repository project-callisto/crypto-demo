Callisto: Tech to combat sexual assault and harassment

Cryptographic Approach

This site is intended to illustrate select components of Callisto's cryptographic approach. It is not a full demonstration of the product, nor an official reporting platform for victims of sexual assault and harassment. Inputted data will not be stored or communicated outside of the user's browser.

For more information, please visit projectcallisto.org
